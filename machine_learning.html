Descriptions

For my CPSC 483: Machine Learning, my partner and I will use supervised learning to discover what parenting habits cause some behavioral characteristics in their child. We modeled 4 different classifers with RF, KNN, SVM, and AdaBoost to find the best performing model to accurately classify our target labels. For our problem, we had 4 different target labels, each being a multi-class, and after testing each classifer with each target label, the RF has the best performance. After determing the best classifer for each target label, we performed permutation importance for the RF models to determine which are the top 10 descriptive features that has the most influnence on their respective target label.


Technologies

We used python along with ML libraries such as scikit-learn, matplotib, pandas, and numpy.


Challenges

Datasaet preprocessing was the most challenging, because the methodology we take to apporach this process will greatly impact our results. About 80% of our time was primarly focused on this process, as we had to fix the naming conventions of the features, determine the minimum percentage of missing values a datapoint to remove from the dataset, how to fix imbalanced classes, how to perform encoding for categorical varaibel, and etc.


Outcomes

Successlly, we were able to incorprate 4 differenct classifers, and 3 out of the 4 classifers had performance ranging from 80% to 90% for recall, precision, f1-score, and accuracy.
